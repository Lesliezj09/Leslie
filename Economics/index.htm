Biography

Leslie J. Zhang obtained his B.A. in Korean from the Jilin University of Finance and Economic in 2015. He received his M.A. in Economics from Kyunghee University in 2017. He is currently a PhD Candidate in the Department of Economics of Korea University.

His research focuses on economics growth, human capital, inequality and the aging of the population. His research has been published in peer-reviewed journals including Journal of Social Science(in Korean).
